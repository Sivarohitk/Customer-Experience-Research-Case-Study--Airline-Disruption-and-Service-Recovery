# Visualization Figure Guide

The visualization step creates the following figures in `outputs/figures/`:

- `Legacy disruption summary`: `disruption_type_counts.png`
- `Top customer pain points`: `theme_counts.png`
- `Overall sentiment distribution`: `sentiment_distribution.png`
- `Negative sentiment by theme`: `negative_sentiment_by_theme.png`
- `Common pain-point combinations`: `pain_point_combinations.png`
- `Observed rating distribution`: `rating_distribution.png`
- `Negative review terms and phrases`: `top_terms.png`
- `Summary dashboard`: `cx_research_dashboard.png`
