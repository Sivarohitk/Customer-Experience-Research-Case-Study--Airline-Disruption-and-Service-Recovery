# Resume Project Summary

- Designed and shipped a mixed-methods UX/CX research pipeline in Python to analyze 41,790 airline feedback records across 370 airlines.
- Built reproducible workflows for public-data ingestion, synthetic fallback generation, preprocessing, rule-based theme coding, sentiment scoring, quantitative cross-analysis, and matplotlib visualizations.
- Identified the dominant disruption themes in customer feedback, led by delay frustration (8,200 reviews, 19.6%) and baggage problems (6,316 reviews, 15.1%).
- Quantified downstream loyalty risk by showing that trust / loyalty damage carried 95.2% negative sentiment, while the most common co-occurring pain point was delay frustration + baggage problems (1,600 reviews).
